Grossmont Cybersecurity Club is a student-led organization focused on providing a community for students in the Grossmont-Cuyamaca Community College District who are interested in cybersecurity. Our club aims to offer opportunities for members to gain practical skills, attend industry events, and build relationships with like-minded individuals. We launched this archival website to keep track of our activities for the benefit of current and future members, with the goal of building a lasting legacy for the club.
